Mental Health Awareness

Understanding, Managing, and Supporting Mental Health.

How is your mental health?

Awareness is the first step to change
Do I feel sad?

  • Do you experience feelings of emptiness or hopelessness?
  • Are you irritable or anxious?
  • Do these feelings come and go or persist for long periods of time?

Am I lonely?

  • Have you lost interest or pleasure in activities you once enjoyed?
  • Do you avoid contact with friends and family
  • Do social gatherings make you anxious

Am I tired?

  • Do simple tasks make you tired?
  • Do you have trouble falling asleep at night?
  • Have you lost your appetite?
